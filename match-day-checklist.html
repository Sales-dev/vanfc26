Match Day Checklist

Everything you need before heading to BC Place

The Night Before

  • ✅ Pack your clear bag — max 12" × 6" × 12". Test it at home before you go.
  • ✅ Check the Canada Line and Expo Line schedule on the transit guide — remember Stadium–Chinatown station is closed on match days
  • ✅ Load your Compass Card — aim for $10+ to cover return fare
  • ✅ Screenshot or download your match ticket — don't rely on WiFi at the gate
  • ✅ Charge your phone to 100%
  • ✅ Check the weather — evenings can be cool even in July. Bring a light layer.
  • ✅ Book your post-match bar or restaurant if you want a guaranteed table

What to Bring

  • ✅ Match ticket — on phone or printed
  • ✅ Clear bag (max 12" × 6" × 12") or small clutch (max 4.5" × 6.5")
  • ✅ Photo ID — occasionally checked at entry
  • ✅ Compass Card with enough credit loaded
  • ✅ Cash and card — food and drinks inside BC Place accept both, but lines for card payments can be long
  • ✅ National team kit or scarf — the more visible the better
  • ✅ Light jacket or layer — BC Place has air conditioning and evenings cool down
  • ✅ Portable charger — if it fits in your clear bag
  • ✅ Earplugs — optional but the crowd at BC Place gets loud under the roof

What NOT to Bring

  • ❌ Backpacks (any size, any colour)
  • ❌ Large purses or tote bags
  • ❌ Outside food or drink (sealed water 500ml may be permitted — confirm with FIFA)
  • ❌ Professional cameras with detachable lenses
  • ❌ Noisemakers, horns, or instruments (unless specifically approved)
  • ❌ Flags on poles longer than 1 metre
  • ❌ Political messaging or banners
  • ❌ Laser pointers

Getting There — Match Day Timeline

  • 3 hours before kickoff: Leave your accommodation. On Canada match days, transit fills extremely fast.
  • 2.5 hours before: Gates open. Getting in early means shorter queues at security, food, and merchandise.
  • 90 minutes before: If you haven't left yet, Uber/Lyft surge pricing will be high. Walk or take SkyTrain.
  • 60 minutes before: Recommended latest arrival to clear security without stress.
  • At Main Street–Science World station: Exit and follow the crowd west on Terminal Avenue. It's a 15-minute flat walk.

After the Match

  • ✅ Don't rush — the exits flood immediately. Stay in your seat for 5–10 minutes after the whistle.
  • ✅ Walk to a bar in Yaletown or Gastown rather than trying to get transit immediately — wait 30–45 min for crowds to clear.
  • ✅ Rideshare surges will be extreme for at least an hour post-match. Plan for this.
  • ✅ If you need the SkyTrain, Main Street–Science World will be packed — be patient and let a few trains pass if needed.
