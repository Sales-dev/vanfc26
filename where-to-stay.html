Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book my hotel for World Cup 2026 in Vancouver?

As early as possible — ideally before April 2026. Vancouver has limited hotel inventory and prices will spike as the tournament approaches. Hotels near BC Place and in Yaletown/Gastown book out months in advance for major events.

Which neighbourhood is closest to BC Place?

Yaletown is the closest neighbourhood, a 10–15 minute walk south along Pacific Blvd. Stadium–Chinatown SkyTrain station is a 5-minute walk from BC Place — so any hotel near a SkyTrain stop is effectively close to the stadium.

Is it cheaper to stay in Richmond?

Yes, Richmond hotels are typically 20–40% cheaper than downtown Vancouver. The Canada Line SkyTrain connects Richmond to downtown in about 20 minutes, making it a practical budget option — especially for fans attending multiple matches.

Can I use Airbnb for World Cup 2026 in Vancouver?

Yes, but book very early. Airbnb and short-term rentals are available in Vancouver, and a full apartment can be more economical for groups of 3–4 people than multiple hotel rooms. Ensure your listing is compliant with Vancouver's short-term rental regulations.
