Key Locations at a Glance

🏟️ BC Place

Match venue for all 6 Vancouver group stage games + 1 knockout round. Capacity 54,500.

Nearest transit: Stadium–Chinatown SkyTrain (5 min walk)

Expo LineMillennium Line

🎪 PNE Fan Festival

Official FIFA Fan Festival at Hastings Park. Giant screens, live entertainment, cultural activations, food vendors, merchandise.

Getting there: Expo Line to Renfrew, then bus 14 east

Free entryAll ages

🚇 Canada Line to Downtown

YVR Airport → Waterfront in ~25 min. Key stops: Richmond–Brighouse, Bridgeport, Vancouver City Centre, Waterfront.

Transfer at Waterfront for Expo Line to Stadium–Chinatown.

Airport$4.55 fare

🍺 Gastown Watch Zone

Steam Clock area and Water Street bars open early on match days. The Blarney Stone, Chill Winston, and pub terraces fill fast — arrive 90 min early for a seat.

Neighbourhood Guides

Gastown

  • 5 min from BC Place by foot or SkyTrain
  • Cobblestone streets, Victorian brick pubs
  • Best for pre-match drinks and post-match celebrations
  • Watch Steam Clock at Cambie & Water St

Yaletown

  • 10 min walk from BC Place, south along Pacific Blvd
  • Upscale restaurants, rooftop patios, craft cocktails
  • Best for post-match dinner or quieter watch parties
  • False Creek seawall views

Commercial Drive

  • Take Expo Line to Commercial–Broadway, walk north
  • Multi-cultural cafes, Italian bakeries, international bars
  • Best for Brazil, Italy, Portugal fan atmospheres
  • Café Calabria, The Charlatan, The Anza Club

Richmond

  • Canada Line: 20 min from downtown
  • Largest Asian food scene in North America
  • Aberdeen Centre, Parker Place food courts
  • Late-night dim sum, bubble tea, BBQ pork buns

SkyTrain Lines at a Glance

Expo Line (Yellow/Blue)

Waterfront → King George. Key stops for fans: Waterfront, Granville, Stadium–Chinatown, Main St–Science World, Commercial–Broadway, Renfrew (PNE).

⚠ Closures possible on Sundays Jun–Jul — check TransLink before match day.

Canada Line (Teal)

YVR Airport → Waterfront. Key stops: YVR-Airport, Bridgeport, Richmond–Brighouse, Vancouver City Centre, Waterfront. Transfer at Waterfront for Expo Line.

Airport connector

Millennium Line (Yellow)

VCC–Clark → Lafarge Lake–Douglas. Shares Stadium–Chinatown with Expo Line. Use for arriving from Burnaby and Coquitlam.

Shares BC Place stop

SeaBus + West Coast Express

SeaBus runs Waterfront → Lonsdale Quay (North Vancouver) every 15–30 min. WCE Commuter rail from Mission → Waterfront on weekdays — check schedule as it's limited on weekends.
