Getting Tickets

Official FIFA Ticketing

All tickets are sold through FIFA’s official platform only. Group stage sales are largely complete — check official resale. Knockout round tickets go on sale per round as teams are confirmed. Watch for last-minute releases on match day.

🎟 FIFA Official Tickets 📋 Full FIFA Fixture List 🏫 BC Place Official Site

Avoid Ticket Scams

  • Buy only from FIFA.com/tickets or officially authorised resellers
  • Never purchase from strangers outside BC Place on match day
  • Third-party resale sites carry high risk of fake or cancelled tickets
  • Tickets are fully digital — protect your FIFA account login

No Ticket? Still Worth the Trip

Watch every match live on giant screens at the FIFA Fan Festival at the PNE — free entry. Or catch the atmosphere at one of Vancouver’s top World Cup sports bars, many with dedicated match-day setups near BC Place.
